To reduce size:
mogrify -resize 50% *jpg

To rename test:
rename 's/.+/our $i; sprintf("Y%03d.jpg", 1+$i++)/e' * -vn

To rename in fact remove '-vn'

ffmpeg -framerate 2 -i Y%03d.jpg -c:v libx264 -profile:v high -crf 20 -pix_fmt yuv420p output-2-fps.mp4
